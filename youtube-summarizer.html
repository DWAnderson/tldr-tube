Summary

How to share YouTube videos directly to this app:

1
Open this page in Chrome on Android
2
Tap Chrome's ⋮ menu → "Add to Home screen"
3
Tap Add — TLDR Tube is now installed as an app
4
In the YouTube app, open any video
5
Tap Share → TLDR Tube in the share sheet
6
The app opens and starts summarizing automatically ✓

The Web Share Target feature requires the app to be installed (Added to Home Screen) in Chrome. Once installed, TLDR Tube appears in Android's share sheet alongside other apps.
